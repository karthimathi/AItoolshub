Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, and any other information you voluntarily provide.

Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you interact with our AI tools, including IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited.

AI Interaction Data: We may collect prompts, inputs, and outputs generated through our AI tools to improve our services, but we anonymize this data where possible.

2

How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our AI tools and services.

Communication: To send important updates, security alerts, and support messages.

Personalization: To customize your experience and show relevant AI tool recommendations.

Analytics: To analyze usage patterns and optimize our platform performance.

Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

3

Data Sharing & Disclosure

Service Providers: We share data with trusted third-party providers who assist in operating our platform (hosting, analytics, payment processing).

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred.

Never Sold: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, firewalls, and secure server infrastructure to protect your data.

Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments are conducted to maintain the highest security standards.

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5

Your Rights & Choices

Access: You can request a copy of your personal data.

Correction: You can update or correct inaccurate information.

Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.

Opt-Out: You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.

Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format.

6

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and deliver personalized content.

Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site.

Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7

Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with third-party services (payment processors, analytics tools, AI model providers) that have their own privacy policies.

We carefully vet third-party providers for privacy compliance but are not responsible for their practices.

When you use integrated services, you may be subject to both our Privacy Policy and the third party's policy.

8

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

Account data is retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward for legal and business purposes.

Usage data is typically anonymized after 24 months, unless needed for security or legal reasons.

You can request earlier deletion of your data through our contact channels.

9

International Data Transfers

As a global platform, your data may be processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers, including standard contractual clauses and adequacy decisions.

By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your data to countries that may have different data protection laws.
